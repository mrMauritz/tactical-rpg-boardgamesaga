# The Saga: Archetype Matrix

This matrix details the **36 distinct character identities** possible in *The Saga*. A character's deepest nature is defined by three overlapping choices:
1.  **The Attribute Domain:** Which of the 6 core stats they rely on.
2.  **The Architecture (Lá vs. Óðr):** Do they anchor in the physical body, or open their mind to magic?
3.  **The Alignment / Source (Order vs. Wild vs. Chaos):** Do they submit to laws, harmonize with nature, or break the rules?

By multiplying the 6 Attributes by the 6 Alignment-Pillar paths, the world generates these specific cultural identities.

---

## 1. Strength (The Domain of Mass & Gravity)

### The Physical Pillar: *The Center* (Mastery of the Axis)
*   **ORDER (The Champion): The Iron-Sworn.** Warriors forged by divine oaths. They are unshakeable living fortresses who guard temples and kings.
*   **WILD (The Beast-Marked): The Boulder-Kin.** Feral muscle. They rely on sheer, untamed physical density achieved through mountain exile.
*   **CHAOS (The Godless): The Sundered.** Unrelenting destructors. They have cracked their own spines through brutal over-training to achieve terrifying, reality-breaking force.

### The Mystic Pillar: *The Root* (Mastery of Weight)
*   **ORDER (The Pious Conduit): The Earth-Priest.** Sanctioned clerics who wield Thor’s gravity to pin enemies to the dirt.
*   **WILD (The Animist): The Quake-Caller.** Mountain shamans who negotiate with the stone, making the earth shudder through drum and blood. 
*   **CHAOS (The Sejd-Thief): The Gravity-Thief.** Outlaw wizards who hack the Wyrd to invert mass, hurling enemies into the air or collapsing their ribs.

---

## 2. Dexterity (The Domain of Precision & Tension)

### The Physical Pillar: *The Web* (Mastery of Fascia)
*   **ORDER (The Champion): The Silk-Sworn.** Impeccable duelists and divine executioners whose movements are geometrically flawless.
*   **WILD (The Beast-Marked): The Viper-Marked.** Instinctual dancers who move like water, adapting perfectly to the immediate tactical terrain.
*   **CHAOS (The Godless): The Ghost-Walker.** Assassins who move without Wyrd-presence. They are completely sealed off from reality, creating no noise and no spiritual ripple.

### The Mystic Pillar: *The Loom* (Mastery of Threads)
*   **ORDER (The Pious Conduit): The Norn-Speaker.** Sanctioned weavers who tug on the golden threads of fate, providing tactical fortune to allies.
*   **WILD (The Animist): The Wind-Dancer.** Nomads who bleed into the breeze, riding physical gusts to traverse impossible gaps.
*   **CHAOS (The Sejd-Thief): The Thread-Cutter.** Witch-assassins who slice the metaphysical kinetic connections between an enemy's brain and their limbs.

---

## 3. Intelligence (The Domain of Science & The Stream)

### The Physical Pillar: *The Pulse* (Mastery of Neurology)
*   **ORDER (The Champion): The Calculated.** Generals and master-at-arms who operate on perfect, frictionless synaptic efficiency. No wasted motion.
*   **WILD (The Beast-Marked): The Instinct-Bred.** Survivors who have bypassed high cognition entirely. They act before they think, driven by hyper-evolved reflexes.
*   **CHAOS (The Godless): The Severed-Nerve.** Warriors who have completely decoupled their pain receptors. They move with erratic, terrifying speed unhindered by biological limits.

### The Mystic Pillar: *The Seer* (Mastery of Analytical Magic)
*   **ORDER (The Pious Conduit): The Truth-Seer.** Astrologers and deep-lore mages who perfectly map the divine equations of the Wyrd.
*   **WILD (The Animist): The Dream-Walker.** Those who navigate the hidden architecture of the deep woods through hallucinogenic trance rather than scrolls. 
*   **CHAOS (The Sejd-Thief): The Paradox.** Abyssal hackers who use logic loops to crash reality, causing spatial anomalies and localized blinding.

---

## 4. Endurance (The Domain of Marrow & Vessels)

### The Physical Pillar: *The Marrow* (Mastery of Adaptation)
*   **ORDER (The Champion): The Anvil.** The chosen anvil of the Gods. They can safely absorb magical impacts without structural failure.
*   **WILD (The Beast-Marked): The Troll-Blooded.** Barbarians whose bodies physically mutate (e.g., bone thickening) the longer a fight goes on.
*   **CHAOS (The Godless): The Un-Dying.** Nihilists whose sheer refusal to submit to death overrides their biology. They continue fighting at 0 HP through sheer sovereign grit.

### The Mystic Pillar: *The Well* (Capacity for the Wyrd)
*   **ORDER (The Pious Conduit): The Chalice.** Holy relics in human form. Their massive internal capacity allows them to host divine miracles without shattering.
*   **WILD (The Animist): The Spirit-Host.** Mediums whose bodies act as a temporary meat-suit for feral spirits, changing their physical properties in combat.
*   **CHAOS (The Sejd-Thief): The Abyssal Drinker.** Warlocks who survive extreme Wyrd-Friction by venting magical radiation into the earth beneath them, often poisoning it.

---

## 5. Willpower (The Domain of Faith & Pressure)

### The Physical Pillar: *The Breath* (Mastery of Internal Chemistry)
*   **ORDER (The Champion): The Iron-Lung.** Stoic champions who use chanted breath algorithms to perfectly manage their stamina (SP) across multi-day sieges. 
*   **WILD (The Beast-Marked): The Roaring-Lung.** Primal warriors (like the classic Berserker) whose screams alter their physiological pain tolerance. 
*   **CHAOS (The Godless): The Gasping.** Terrifying duelists who fight in total silence, dropping their heart rate to practically zero to become invisible to heat and sound tracking.

### The Mystic Pillar: *The Word* (Mastery of the Decree)
*   **ORDER (The Pious Conduit): The Law-Speaker.** Paladins and High-Gothis enforcing Odin's decree. When they mandate reality, it builds static truth (buffing allies).
*   **WILD (The Animist): The Curse-Singer.** Tribal mystics who weave hexes through raw emotion and guttural rhythm.
*   **CHAOS (The Sejd-Thief): The Doom-Caster.** The heretics who weaponize fear, screaming forbidden syllables to cause biological panic and rout formations.

---

## 6. Charisma (The Domain of Nature & Presence)

### The Physical Pillar: *The Attention* (Mastery of The Gaze)
*   **ORDER (The Champion): The Warlord.** Their mere physical presence (The Right Hemisphere 'Master') brings order to chaotic battlefields, anchoring the morale of all adjacent allies.
*   **WILD (The Beast-Marked): The Alpha.** The pack leader. Without speaking, they coordinate flanking maneuvers of hunting squads through dominant body language alone. 
*   **CHAOS (The Godless): The Terror-Gaze.** The lone predator. The intensity of their sovereign isolation breaks the spirit (causes paralysis or hesitation) of anyone who locks eyes with them.

### The Mystic Pillar: *The Shadow* (Mastery of the Bridge)
*   **ORDER (The Pious Conduit): The Spirit-Warden.** Holy diplomats who negotiate clean passage for souls and establish safe wards against the undead.
*   **WILD (The Animist): The Blood-Weaver.** Blood-magic users who project their life-force outward, sharing their HP/SP pools with allies or bonded beasts. 
*   **CHAOS (The Sejd-Thief): The Soul-Flayer.** Necromancers and mind-invaders who force their dominating presence into the minds of enemies to puppet them.
