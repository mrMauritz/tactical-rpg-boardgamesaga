## Agreement Log: Disabling Arts (Saga & UFC)
*Status: Described & Concept-Approved (Sketching Phase)*

The following techniques were described and agreed upon as the foundation for future **Perks / Skills**:

1.  **The Clinch (The Iron Anchor)**:
    *   **Logic**: Anchoring mass to the opponent's **Center** (Glima *Broktök*). 
    *   **Agreement**: A specialized skill that links the centers of both combatants. It creates a continuous **BP (Balance Point) Drain** every round. This allows the opponent to naturally reach **Unstable/Stumbling** states without extra status rules.

2.  **The Hook/Pin (The Mail-Troll)**:
    *   **Logic**: Using leverage to restrict the opponent's **Web** (Fascia). 
    *   **Agreement**: A technique enabling a "Hook" on a limb or shield via a Stage 1 success. This disrupts the fascial web, resulting in **Accuracy/Defense penalties** or **Attacker Advantage**, representing the physical restriction of the limb.

3.  **The Intercepting Sweep (Heel-Trip)**:
    *   **Logic**: Timing the **Pulse** to catch the opponent mid-motion (UFC/Glima). 
    *   **Agreement**: A reaction-based technique that catches the opponent at their peak instability, resulting in an **Immediate BP Drop (Unstable)** and the loss of the active segment.

4.  **Systemic Grinder (Total Disruption)**:
    *   **Logic**: Positional dominance (Ground-and-Pound) striking through the **Marrow**.
    *   **Agreement**: A perk for attacking compromised (**Stumbling/Fallen**) targets. It ignores **Natural Armor** and deals direct **SP Damage**. This naturally triggers the **Glance Rule** (Physiological Shock) as the opponent's systemic integrity is crushed.

5.  **Terror-Gaze (The Attention)**:
    *   **Logic**: Using the **Pillar of Attention** (CHA) to exert psychological dominance. 
    *   **Agreement**: A perk allowing a Major Action to force a **Route Check** on unstable enemies. This uses the **Reality Core** of morale collapse rather than magic.
    
6.  **Law-Speaker (The Word)**:
    *   **Logic**: Using the **Pillar of The Word** (WIL) to resolve conflict through authority. 
    *   **Agreement**: A perk for forcing surrenders on staggered/broken enemies. It rewards characters who develop "The Word" to resolve battles without lethality.

7.  **Shadow-Step (The Web)**:
    *   **Logic**: Using the **Pillar of The Web** (DEX) to manipulate the tactical setup *before* the round begins. 
    *   **Agreement**: A prep-phase perk for the **Infiltration** approach. It allows bypassing a unit, removing it from the initial tactical geometry unless detected.

## Agreement Log: Historical Reaction Identities (The 5 Layers)
*Status: Concept-Approved (Sketching Phase)*

To deepen the "Reaction Identity" of characters, we move beyond generic dodging and anchor reactions in historical tactical laws.

1.  **The Ma-ai (Distance/Spatial Control)**:
    *   **Logic**: Controlling the "Hex Gap." Managing distance so the enemy cannot land a clean blow.
    *   **Anchor**: **DEX (The Web)** + **INT (The Pulse)**.
    *   **Reaction Identity**: *The Barrier-Master*. Their reaction allows them to move 1 Hex backward/sideways *during* the attacker's approach, potentially negating the strike.

2.  **The Frame-Breaker (Structural Collapse)**:
    *   **Logic**: Collapsing the opponent's spine/frame (Glima/Wrestling).
    *   **Anchor**: **STR (The Center)** + **BP (Balance Points)**.
    *   **Reaction Identity**: *The Grappler-King*. They step *into* the strike to "bind" the attacker, dealing immediate **BP Damage** and potentially making them *Stumbling* mid-action.

3.  **The Intercept (The First-Strike Reaction)**:
    *   **Logic**: Striking the *intent* (Bruce Lee's Intercepting Fist / Musashi's *Sen-no-sen*).
    *   **Anchor**: **INT (The Pulse)** + **Initiative**.
    *   **Reaction Identity**: *The Counter-Striker*. A "Stop-Hit" that attempts to strike during the opponent's attack, interrupting their action entirely if successful.

4.  **The Siege-Lungs (Metabolic Attrition)**:
    *   **Logic**: Out-breathing the enemy. Exhausting their capacity to act.
    *   **Anchor**: **WIL (The Breath)** + **END (The Marrow)**.
    *   **Reaction Identity**: *The Relentless*. They absorb the blow (trading HP) but use **The Breath** to immediately recover **SP (Stamina)**, waiting for the opponent to "Gas Out" (0 SP).

5.  **The Moral Gravity (The Flinch-Response)**:
    *   **Logic**: Weaponizing psychology to cause a neurological "Flinch" (Kuzushi).
    *   **Anchor**: **CHA (The Attention)** + **WIL (The Word)**.
    *   **Reaction Identity**: *The Warlord/Terror*. Using a "Reaction Shout" or "Death Gaze" to force an attacker to re-roll their 3d6, representing momentary hesitation.

## Deep-Dive: Missing Historical Identities (Expanded)
*Status: Identity Foundations*

### 1. The "Gallowglass" (The Lever-Master)
*   **The Historical Fact:** High-tier warriors used the **Leverage of the Shaft**. A poleaxe or Dane-axe is a lever, a hook, and a spear.
*   **The Reaction (The Bind):** Instead of parrying, use the length of the weapon to "pin" the attacker's weapon against their own body.
*   **Mechanical Result:** Creates a **Structural Entrapment**. The attacker is "Locked" and cannot use that weapon until they spend a Major Action to "Break the Bind" (STR vs STR).
*   **Pillar Anchor:** **STR (The Center)** + **INT (The Pulse)**.

### 2. The "Heihō" Deceiver (The Architect of the Falsehood)
*   **The Historical Fact:** Masters like Musashi taught the **"Empty and the Full."** You show a "False Opening" to dictate where the enemy *must* strike.
*   **The Reaction (The Gambit):** The character *intentionally* lowers their BP (Balance) to "Unstable" during the opponent's turn to lure a "Commitment Strike."
*   **Mechanical Result:** Once the opponent commits segments, the Deceiver uses **Attention (CHA)** to "Void" the hex, leaving the attacker over-extended and facing a 180-degree disadvantage.
*   **Pillar Anchor:** **CHA (The Attention)** + **DEX (The Web)**.

### 3. The "Huskarl" (The Wyrd-Link)
*   **The Historical Fact:** The Viking Shield-Wall was a **Unified Organism**.
*   **The Reaction (The Interpose):** If an adjacent ally is attacked, the Huskarl "Steps into the Gap."
*   **Mechanical Result:** They **Share the Architecture**. The ally uses the Huskarl's **END (Marrow)** bonus for their defense. This turns the hex-grid into a "Circuit" of protection.
*   **Pillar Anchor:** **WIL (The Word)** + **STR (The Center)**.

## The "Brilliant" Standard for Character Paths
*Status: Design Rubric*

Strategic paths in BGS are **Systemic Evolutions**, not flat numerical bonuses.

*   **Low Standard:** "You get +2 to parry."
*   **BGS Brilliant Standard:** "You have unlocked **The Kinetic Mirror**. You no longer 'Block'; you 'Store.' When you are hit while Stable, you gain +1 IS Die for every 2 damage absorbed, which must be spent on your next strike."
