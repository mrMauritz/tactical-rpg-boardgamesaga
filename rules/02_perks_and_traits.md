# PART II — PERKS & TRAITS

## Chapter 3 — The Organic Growth Policy
Perks in *BoardGameSaga* are not bulk-designed as a prerequisite for play. They are **System Alterations** that emerge naturally from the tactical needs of the core battle system. New perks are added to this document only when a unique mechanical solution is required for a specific class, monster, or tactical scenario.

---

## Chapter 4 — Tactical Perks (Tier 1)
These perks modify the fundamental mathematics of the **Battle Core**.

### Unyielding
The character possesses an unnatural font of stamina or relentless drill.
- **Effect:** Increase the character's **SP Regeneration Rate** by **+1**.

### Hard Head
The character is a veteran of heavy impacts or possesses a thick skeletal frame.
- **Effect:** When **Stunned**, the character only loses **2 Segments** of their next action budget (instead of 3). Furthermore, they may still perform **Major Actions** while stunned.

### Nerves of Steel
The character maintains their defensive posture even when physically exhausted.
- **Effect:** When **SP = 0 (Gassed)**, the character's **Defense** is **halved** rather than being reduced to 0. They maintain the ability to form a basic guard, though they remains unable to take active reactions until recovery.

## Chapter 4.1 — Precision Perks (Precision Impacts)
These perks allow a character to perform a **Precision Effect Swap** on high-quality hits, trading raw kinetic power (IS Dice) for specialized anatomical or tactical results (**Precision Effects**).

### Concussive Blow

*Prerequisite: Heavy/Crushing Weapon, Skill 3+.*
- **Effect:** On a **Great/Perfect Success (18+)** or **Natural 18**, the attacker may perform a **Precision Effect Swap**, trading their IS bonus (+1/+2 Dice) for a **Concussion** (Automatic Stun) **Precision Effect**.

### Surgical Edge
*Prerequisite: Sharp/Piercing Weapon, Skill 3+.*
- **Effect:** On a **Great/Perfect Success (18+)** or **Natural 18**, the attacker may perform a **Precision Effect Swap**, trading their IS bonus (+1/+2 Dice) for the **Hit the Gap** (Total Armor Bypass) **Precision Effect**.

### Hamstring
*Prerequisite: Sharp Weapon, Skill 3+.*
- **Effect:** On a **Great/Perfect Success (18+)** or **Natural 18**, the attacker may perform a **Precision Effect Swap**, trading their IS bonus (+1/+2 Dice) for the **Sever Tendon** **Precision Effect**. Target's Movement is reduced to 0 for 1 round.

### Eye-Shot
*Prerequisite: Archery, Skill 5+.*
- **Effect:** On a **Perfect Success (20+)** or **Natural 18**, the attacker may perform a **Precision Effect Swap**, trading their IS bonus (+2 Dice) for the **Blinded** **Precision Effect** (-2 Accuracy to target).
