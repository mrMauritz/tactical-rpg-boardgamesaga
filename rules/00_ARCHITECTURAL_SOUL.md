# The Saga: The Architectural Soul

> **READ THIS FIRST** at the start of every session. This is the "Vision Anchor" that prevents AI regression and rule-bloat.

1. **NEAT & CLEAN**: If a mechanic adds a new roll or a new modifier, it has failed.
2. **CORE-FIRST**: Every tactical depth must be an EXTENSION of the existing 3-Stage Pipeline (**Relative Force** -> **Success Ladder** -> **Result Swap**), not a new layer.
3. **RESULT-SWAP (Pinnacle Perks)**: High-tier success (**Great 18+**, **Perfect 20+**) "Trades" raw Impact Severity (IS) for Special Effects (Broken Arm, Stunned, Bypass). No additional math allowed.
4. **PLAYER-DRIVEN GEOMETRY**: Tactical advantage comes from Hex-Positions (Formations) and situational awareness, not stat-sheet bonuses.
5. **ORGANIC GROWTH**: Do not pre-design. Perks, Tiers, and Abilities emerge naturally from tactical needs during development. We build the skeleton (Traditions/Paths) and grow the flesh (Tiers) as needed.
6. **PHYSICAL REALISM**: If it doesn't make sense in a collision between two physical bodies, it doesn't belong in the core.
7. **NO PLACEHOLDERS**: Every rule must be a working tool, not a placeholder for "future work."
8. **UNIFIED CONSTITUTION**: The computer version (`thesaga`) and the board game (`boardgamesaga`) share the same mechanical soul. Any deviation is a bug.
9. **DETAIL PRESERVATION (NO PRUNING)**: Consolidation is NOT summarization. Approved deep-dives (Pillar mastery, Methodologies, Equations) are **Permanent Historical Records**. Never delete or "simplify" them in the name of neatness. If a document's line count shrinks during a revision, perform an immediate **Detail Integrity Audit** to ensure no "Flesh" was lost from the "Skeleton."
