# Rule of Wild: 12 Mutations (WIP)

The mutations now follow a strict **Law of Inversion**. 
If a Physical Wild gives a (+X) benefit to your physical attacks, it applies a (-X) penalty to your magical casting. The specifics below strictly use the new mechanical values.

## 1. Strength (STR) - Force & Impact

### Physical Wild: *The Jötunn-Grip*
*Your physical mass hardens like stone, channeling all force into kinetic strikes while hollowly rejecting magical weight.*
*   **Evolution (Lá):** Add +1d6 to your IS (Impact Strike) when fighting physically.
*   **Trade-off (Óðr):** If a spell is used, subtract -1d6 from its IS (Impact Strike).

### Mystic Wild: *The Mountain’s Weight*
*Magic solidifies into crushing physical impacts, leaving the caster's own bones remarkably porous and brittle.*
*   **Evolution (Óðr):** Add +1d6 to the IS (Impact Strike) when casting spells.
*   **Trade-off (Lá):** Subtract -1d6 from your physical IS (Impact Strike) when fighting.

---

## 2. Dexterity (DEX) - Speed & Action Economy

### Physical Wild: *The Wind-Walker*
*The body slips through time and space with unmatched speed, dragging behind the sluggish flow of the Wyrd.*
*   **Evolution (Lá):** Add one segment to your turn when fighting.
*   **Trade-off (Óðr):** If spells are used, subtract one segment from your turn.

### Mystic Wild: *The Ghost-Step*
*The mind weaves spells at the speed of thought, untethering the physical body and slowing its gait.*
*   **Evolution (Óðr):** Add one segment to your turn when spells are used.
*   **Trade-off (Lá):** If fighting, subtract one segment from your turn.

---

## 3. Intelligence (INT) - Range & Precision

### Physical Wild: *The Wolf's Focus*
*Physical senses sharpen to predatory distances, entirely blinding the inner eye to the metaphysical stream.*
*   **Evolution (Lá):** Treat your physical weapon range as +1 tier.
*   **Trade-off (Óðr):** If spells are used, subtract -1 range tier from the spell.

### Mystic Wild: *The Raven's Eye*
*Magical sight expands across the horizon, leaving the physical eyes dull and slow to react in melee.*
*   **Evolution (Óðr):** Treat your spell range as +1 tier.
*   **Trade-off (Lá):** Subtract -1 range tier from your physical weapons when fighting.

---

## 4. Endurance (END) - Duration & Adaptation

### Physical Wild: *The Troll-Bark*
*The flesh adapts and regenerates with brutal efficiency, sacrificing the body's internal vessel designed to hold magical energy.*
*   **Evolution (Lá):** SP (Stamina) generation is +1.
*   **Trade-off (Óðr):** MG (Megin) generation is -1.

### Mystic Wild: *The Well-Drinker*
*The soul constantly drafts deep from the Wyrd, leaving the physical body starved of normal metabolic stamina.*
*   **Evolution (Óðr):** MG (Megin) generation is +1.
*   **Trade-off (Lá):** SP (Stamina) generation is -1.

---

## 5. Willpower (WIL) - Penetration & Resistance

### Physical Wild: *The Bear's Roar*
*Shouts and commands shatter the air with absolute authority, tearing the throat and ruining the delicate frequencies of incantations.*
*   **Evolution (Lá):** When fighting, the required cost for HP, SP, or BP is -2.
*   **Trade-off (Óðr):** When casting spells, MP 2 is treated as MP=0 (requiring 2 extra MP to cast effectively).

### Mystic Wild: *The Riven Word*
*The chanted word cuts smoothly through the fabric of reality with ease, causing the physical lungs to stutter and gasp when exerting force.*
*   **Evolution (Óðr):** When casting spells, the required cost for MP (Megin) is -2.
*   **Trade-off (Lá):** When fighting, HP, SP, or BP requirements of 2 are treated as 0 (requiring 2 extra points to act effectively).

---

## 6. Charisma (CHA) - Targets & Chaining

### Physical Wild: *The Pack's Blood*
*The alpha's presence physically dominates and inspires the entire group, completely rejecting the subtle bonds of magical spirits.*
*   **Evolution (Lá):** Leadership benefits you grant physically are doubled.
*   **Trade-off (Óðr):** If spells are used, Party Benefits from those spells are halved.

### Mystic Wild: *The Woven Thread*
*Spiritual resonance naturally flows through all nearby allies, detaching the mind from grounded, tactical leadership.*
*   **Evolution (Óðr):** Party Benefits from your spells are doubled.
*   **Trade-off (Lá):** Leadership benefits you grant physically are halved.
